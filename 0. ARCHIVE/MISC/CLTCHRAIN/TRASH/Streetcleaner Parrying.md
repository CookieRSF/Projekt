Streetcleaner Parrying
	- Jackhammer-Style Offensive Parry (3 Charges)
		- More charge = stronger parry, increased vulnerability. Longer preparation. Rewards delayed parries & predictions. Provides instant protection.
		- Can fire a projectile/core.
		1. Instant Parry: Neutralizes projectiles. Can parry & redirect hitscan shots within very tricky/frame-perfect timing (or within a hitstop). Hitstop redirection applies (hitscan only).
		2. Neutral Parry: Standard redirection. No hitstop redirection.
		3. Full Parry: Full strength. Increases damage. Hit-stop redirection applies. Converts projectiles into parryable hitscan shots. Provides offensive buffs.
	- Feedbacker-Style Defensive Parry (3 Charges)
		- More charge = weaker parry, decreased vulnerability. Quicker preparation. Rewards instant parries & reactions. Provides delayed protection.
		- Can shoot flames.
		1. Instant Parry: Full strength. Restores health. Parries projectiles. Can parry & redirect hitscan shots within very tricky/frame-perfect timing (or within a hitstop). Hitstop redirection applies (projectiles & hitscan).
		2. Neutral Parry: Standard redirection. No hitstop redirection.
		3. Full Parry: Neutralizes projectiles. Provides defensive buffs.
	- A parry exchange may continue infinitely.
	- Hitscan shots caught in hitstop are given immediately visible trails/tracers.
